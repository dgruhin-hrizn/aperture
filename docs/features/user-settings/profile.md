# Profile Settings

Profile settings manage your account information, email, and notification preferences.

## Accessing Profile Settings

1. Click your avatar in the top navigation bar
2. Select **Settings**
3. Click the **Profile** tab

---

## Account Information

### Username

Your username from Emby/Jellyfin:

- **Display only** — Cannot be changed in Aperture
- Used for login
- Shown in your personal library names

### Avatar

Your profile picture:

- Synced from your media server
- Displayed in the top bar and comments
- Updates automatically when changed in Emby/Jellyfin

---

## Email Address

### Purpose

Your email is captured for:

- Future notification features (coming soon)
- Account recovery (future)
- Communication from your admin (optional)

### Setting Your Email

1. Enter your email address in the field
2. Click **Save** or the field auto-saves

### Auto-Sync from Emby Connect

If your Emby username is an email address:

- Email is automatically populated
- Won't be overwritten if you set a different one
- Keeps your media server email in sync

### Privacy

Your email is:

- Stored only in Aperture's database
- Not shared with external services
- Visible only to you and admins

## Email Notifications

**Note:** Email notifications are currently a placeholder feature for future development.

### Current Functionality

The notification toggle allows you to:

- Express interest in future notifications
- Be ready when the feature launches

### Planned Features

Future notification options may include:

| Notification | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| New recommendations | When fresh picks are generated |
| Trending alerts | Popular content notifications |
| New episodes | When tracked shows have new episodes |
| Request updates | Status changes for Discovery requests |

### Enabling/Disabling

1. Toggle the **Email Notifications** switch
2. Setting is saved automatically
3. Will take effect when features launch

## Account Details

### Member Since

Shows when your Aperture account was created:

- First login date
- Visible for reference

### Last Active

Shows your most recent activity:

- Updates on each session
- Helps track account usage

## Account Actions

### Logout

Sign out of Aperture:

1. Click your avatar
2. Select **Logout**
3. Redirected to login page

Your session is cleared and you'll need to log in again.

### Account Data

**Data portability is managed by your media server:**

- Watch history is in Emby/Jellyfin
- Ratings sync to Trakt if connected
- Aperture preferences are stored separately

---

## Security

### Session Management

Your login session:

- Persists until logout
- Cleared on browser cookie clear
- Can be reset by admin

### Two-Factor Authentication

2FA is handled by your media server:

- If enabled in Emby/Jellyfin, affects Aperture login
- Aperture doesn't have separate 2FA

---

## Tips

### Keep Email Updated

- Ensures you receive future notifications
- Allows admin to contact you if needed
- Required for some future features

### Check Connected Services

- Verify Trakt is connected for rating sync
- Check status periodically
