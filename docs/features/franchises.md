# Franchises

The Franchises page shows movie collections and series universes in your library.

## What Are Franchises?

Franchises are groups of related content:

| Type | Examples |
|------|----------|
| **Movie Collections** | Marvel Cinematic Universe, Star Wars, James Bond |
| **Series Universes** | Breaking Bad + Better Call Saul, Star Trek series |
| **Trilogies** | The Dark Knight Trilogy, Lord of the Rings |

---

## Accessing Franchises

Multiple ways to find franchises:

1. **Search** — Type a franchise name in global search
2. **Movie/Series Detail** — Click the collection link
3. **Browse filters** — Use the Franchise filter on Movies
4. **Direct URL** — `/franchises`

---

## Franchise Page

Each franchise shows:

### Header

- **Franchise name** — Collection title
- **Item count** — Total movies/episodes
- **Your progress** — How much you've watched

### Items List

All items in the franchise, typically in:

- **Release order** — When they came out
- **Chronological order** — Story timeline (if different)

### Per-Item Info

| Info | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Poster** | Visual identification |
| **Title** | Movie or series name |
| **Year** | Release year |
| **Watch status** | Watched, unwatched, in progress |
| **Your rating** | Heart rating if rated |

---

## Franchise Progress

Visual indicators show your progress:

| Status | Indicator |
|--------|-----------|
| **Watched** | ✓ Checkmark |
| **In Progress** | ▶ Partial fill |
| **Unwatched** | Empty |

A progress bar shows overall completion.

---

## Using Franchises

### Complete a Collection

- See what you've watched
- Identify gaps in your viewing
- Watch in order

### Rate Franchise Items

Rate individual movies to:

- Track your favorites in the series
- Influence recommendations for similar franchises
- Compare your ratings to the community

### Discover Related Content

Click through to find:

- Similar franchises
- Shared actors/directors across the franchise
- Spin-offs and related content

---

## Franchises and Recommendations

Franchises affect your recommendations:

### Positive Signals

- High ratings on franchise items → More from similar franchises
- Completing a franchise → Suggestions for similar complete series

### Franchise Weights

In [Watcher Identity Settings](user-settings/watcher-identity.md), you can:

- See detected franchises
- Adjust franchise influence on recommendations
- Exclude franchises you're done with

---

## Large Franchises

For very large franchises (Marvel, Star Wars, etc.):

- Items may be paginated
- Sub-collections may be shown (phases, trilogies)
- Chronological vs release order options
